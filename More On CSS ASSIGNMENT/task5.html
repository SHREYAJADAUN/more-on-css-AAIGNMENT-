1. FONT - ROBOTO

1. Roboto is one of the most famous serif type Google Web Fonts .
2. Can be ideally used for navigation menus, paragraphs, or any content headings .
3. Available in 12 different styles .
4. Roboto also has Roboto Condensed and Roboto Slab in its family .

2. FONT- QUESTRAL

1. Questral is a modern style font that can be used for body paragraph text and headlines .
2. Only available in 1 Style .
3. Has full circle curves in many characters .

3. FONT- POPPINS

1.Poppins is available in 18 different weight style variations .
2.Designed by Indian Type Foundry, this font supports Devanagari and Latin writing systems .
3.Perfect pairing fonts with Poppins are Lato, Raleway, and Oswald .

4. FONT- ARVO

1. Arvo is a geometric slab-serif font with 4 different styles (Regular, Regular Italic, Bold, and Bold Italic) .
2. Suitable for heading text and testimonial paragraphs .
3. Might not work for navigation menus and paragraph text .
